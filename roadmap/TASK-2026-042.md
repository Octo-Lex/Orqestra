## Context
Every commit in Orqestra carries structured semantic metadata: intent summary, affected concepts, confidence score, and a reasoning trace. The git-bridge crate provides `semantic_commit()` and `backfill_semantic_stub()` which POSTs to the AI service's `/extract-intent` endpoint, upgrades Pending stubs to Complete via atomic write-rename, and writes reasoning traces to `.Orqestra/graph/reasoning/`.
