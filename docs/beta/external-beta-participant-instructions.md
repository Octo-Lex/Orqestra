# External Beta Participant Instructions

**Version:** v2.14.0
**Purpose:** Instructions for external beta participants.

---

## What You Are Testing

You are testing the Orqestra beta path — the journey from installing the app to completing onboarding and optionally exporting beta evidence. This session is testing the beta path, not you. A failed, blocked, or incomplete session is still useful evidence.

---

## What to Do

### Step 1: Install or Open Orqestra

- Use the installer or binary provided to you
- If installation fails, that is useful information — please report what happened

### Step 2: Open a Test Repository

- Open a test repository or a repository you are comfortable using for beta testing
- Do not use a repository containing secrets, private data, or sensitive information

### Step 3: Complete the Onboarding/Readiness Flow

- Follow the onboarding steps as far as you can
- If you get stuck or blocked at any step, that is useful information

### Step 4: Export Beta Evidence (Only If You Consent)

- If you reach the readiness panel, you may see an option to export beta evidence
- You may export only if you consent to sharing the exported data
- If you do not consent, that is completely fine — just let us know you declined

### Step 5: Review the Exported Folder Locally

- Before sharing anything, review the exported folder on your computer
- Remove anything you consider private or sensitive
- You are in control of what you share

### Step 6: Share the Folder (Only If Comfortable)

- Share the folder through the method agreed upon with the beta coordinator
- Do not share anything you consider private

---

## Important

- **You may stop at any time.**
- **You may decline evidence export.**
- **Do not share secrets, private code, or sensitive repository data.**
- A blocked or failed session is still valuable — you do not need a perfect run.
- Your identity will not be published. Only aggregate data may appear on the public dashboard.

---

## What Happens with Your Evidence

1. Your bundle is reviewed locally by the beta coordinator
2. Consent and redaction are verified
3. Only aggregate facts (counts, outcomes) may appear publicly
4. Your name, email, repository, and feedback text are never published
5. The raw bundle file is never committed to the repository

---

## Questions?

Contact the beta coordinator through the agreed-upon channel. There are no wrong questions.
